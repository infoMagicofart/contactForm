At our dojo:

At our dojo, we provide comprehensive beginner programs in both disciplines, guiding you through the techniques, etiquette, and philosophy behind these ancient martial arts. Whether you're drawn to the dynamic sparring of Kendo or the precise, meditative movements of Iaido, our experienced instructors will help you develop your skills and deepen your understanding. Join us today to embark on a journey of discipline, focus, and self-improvement through the study of Kendo and Iaido.
